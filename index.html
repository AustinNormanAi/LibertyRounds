Frequently Asked

How does the magnetic cap work?
The cap contains a powerful rare-earth neodymium magnet that locks securely to the carrier body. It holds firm through daily carry, bumps, and impacts, but pops off easily with a thumb push when you need fast access to your rounds.
Will my rounds fall out?
No. The rounds sit in two stacked rings of 5 that keep them snug, and the magnetic cap seals the top. You can shake it, drop it, run with it — your rounds aren't going anywhere until you want them to.
What ammo brands work with it?
LibertyRounds carriers work with all standard 9mm Luger and .45 ACP ammunition from any major manufacturer — Federal, Winchester, Hornady, Speer, Remington, Sig Sauer, and more.
Is it legal to carry?
Yes. The LibertyRounds carrier is simply an ammo storage container. Always check your local and state laws regarding ammunition carry, as regulations vary by jurisdiction.
How is it made?
Each carrier is precision 3D-printed from high-strength polymer in the USA. This allows for tight tolerances, consistent quality, and the ability to produce in both 9mm and .45 ACP configurations with perfect fitment.
