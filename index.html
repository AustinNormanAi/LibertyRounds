Frequently Asked

How strong is the magnetic cap?
Strong. It uses a rare-earth neodymium magnet that holds firm through daily carry, drops, bumps, and bouncing around in your truck. It won't pop open on accident — but one thumb push and it comes right off when you need it.
Will my rounds fall out or rattle?
No. The rounds sit in two stacked rings of 5 that keep them snug, and the magnetic cap seals the top. Shake it, drop it, run with it — dead silent, nothing moves.
What ammo brands work with it?
All of them. Federal, Winchester, Hornady, Speer, Remington, Sig — any standard 9mm Luger or .45 ACP round fits perfectly.
How fast can I access my rounds?
Pull it out, pop the cap with your thumb, and dump. The side window also lets you thumb individual rounds out quickly. Total time from pocket to loaded: seconds.
Is it legal to carry?
It's an ammo storage container — no different than a speed loader or ammo box. Always check your local and state laws regarding ammunition carry.

Orders placed before 2pm EST ship same day. Most domestic orders arrive in 2-4 business days. Free shipping on orders of 3 or more.
