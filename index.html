No. Two stacked rings of 5 keep them snug. Magnetic cap seals the top. Shake it, drop it, run with it — dead silent, nothing moves.

All of them. Federal, Winchester, Hornady, Speer, Remington, Sig — any standard 9mm Luger or .45 ACP round.

Pull it out, pop the cap with your thumb, dump. The side window also lets you thumb rounds out individually. Pocket to loaded in seconds.
